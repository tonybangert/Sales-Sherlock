# Sherlock for teams

The Sherlock CLI is built for individuals. If you want to roll it out across an entire revenue org, you'll likely want some combination of the following — none of which is in the open-source CLI today:

- **Shared prompt libraries** — your team's playbook codified into the prompts, version-controlled, distributed to every rep, updated centrally.
- **Hosted UI** — a web app at your domain so non-technical reps don't touch a terminal. Single sign-on, audit trail, brief history.
- **CRM integration** — auto-write the brief into HubSpot, Salesforce, or Outreach as a contact note, ready before the call.
- **Custom render templates** — your brand, your colors, your logo on every brief, formatted to match how your team already documents accounts.
- **Buying-committee briefs** — research the full account, not just one person. Map the org chart, surface multi-thread strategy.
- **Usage analytics** — see who on your team is actually using it, which briefs are being acted on, which prompts perform best.
- **Compliance and data residency** — keep API calls inside your VPC if you need to.
